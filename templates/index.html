FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is email verification?

    Email verification is the process of checking whether an email address is real, reachable, and safe to send to. Verification and validation are often used interchangeably.

  • Why does email verification matter?

    Invalid and risky addresses drive bounces, spam complaints, and wasted sends. Cleaning your list protects sender reputation and domain health, improves inbox placement, and keeps you from paying to message addresses that will never engage.

  • How quickly will I see deliverability improvements?

    Most will see bounce-rate improvements immediately after switching to cleaned lists. Inbox placement and engagement improvements are dependent on the sending email and campaign, but should improve over time.

  • Do you support both bulk and single email verification?

    Yes, both are supported. Bulk email cleaning is only accessible to our paid plan customers.

  • Can I verify existing lists I already have?

    Yes. Upload existing CSV/TXT lists and run validation right away. You can map columns and export cleaned results for your current ESP or CRM.

  • Is there a limit to the number of emails or lists I can validate?

    No, there is no limit. Once you have the paid plan, you can validate as many emails and lists as needed.

  • How accurate is EmailsConfirmed?

    Our methodology is less conservative than many verification providers. Very strict rules can inflate reported accuracy while excluding legitimate addresses. EmailsConfirmed balances precision with fewer false rejections so valid contacts are not removed unnecessarily.

  • How does the one-time payment work?

    We don't believe in subscriptions, tokens, usage costs, etc. You pay one fee, and no matter how many emails you verify, you don't need to spend another dime.

  • Can I get a refund?

    We offer refunds within 3 days of purchase.
